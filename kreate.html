Welcome to Kre8Art, Your Design and Fabrication House

Background

Kre8Art LLC was established in 2024 as a simple hobby to escape from life's distractions into something creative that would bring joy to others. We have honed our skills along the way in designing as well as creating various forms of merchandise for personal or professional use meant to add warmth, color, and happiness to your surroundings. Few of our favorites are shared below as well as showcased in our picture gallery for you to explore. As we have improved our skills in this past year, we have persuaded more customers to build trust with us. Each of our projects have been a huge success, leading to our massive discovery.

What do we craft here?

    Design and Create:

  1. Decals to personalize products such as notebooks, tumblers, computers, cars, and any other you could think of and the surface and the media can naturally interact.
  2. Artwork for fabrics like T-shirts, hoodies, pillow covers, napkins, aprons, tote bags, keychains, and socks to name a few.
  3. Designs and prints on mugs, tumblers, water bottles, wall arts, luggage tags, laptop skins etc.
